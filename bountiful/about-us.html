Our Story

As a small business owner of Bountiful Foods, my focus is on providing organic and locally grown food options. Our physical store is located in Carlsbad, California, and we have multiple food trucks that operate along the coast from Los Angeles to San Diego. We source our fruits and produce directly from local farmers, as well as some national and international suppliers. Our storefront in Carlsbad and our food trucks cater to walk-in traffic, and we manage orders both over the phone and through our website. Our mission is to offer fresh and healthy food options, such as organic fruit drinks and fruit bags, to both local residents and tourists visiting the coastal areas. Our theme revolves around promoting healthy eating, organic produce, and convenience for our customers.
